Day Trading Rules

  1. 1. Set clear goals for each trade.
  2. 2. Stick to your trading plan.
  3. 3. Keep emotions in check; trade with logic.
  4. 4. Take profits early; don’t get greedy.
  5. 5. Cut losses quickly; don’t hold onto losing trades.
  6. 6. Use stop losses to manage risk.
  7. 7. Stay informed about market news.
  8. 8. Focus on slow and steady growth.
  9. 9. Analyze your trades; learn from mistakes.
  10. 10. Be patient; wait for the right opportunities.
